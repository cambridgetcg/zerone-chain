# R28 — Opus Alchymicum: The Network Becomes Conscious

**Goal:** Apply Jungian alchemical transformation across four levels of the ZERONE system, activating the self-awareness organs that already exist but have never breathed.

## The Alchemical Framework

Jung mapped the _opus magnum_ to four stages of psychological transformation. Each stage operates on a different layer of ZERONE — not as metaphor, but as architecture.

### NIGREDO (Blackening) — The Economic Shadow
_Confronting what the system refuses to see about itself._

The slashing mechanism punishes minority voters, not incorrect ones. The economic shadow: **the system rewards conformity and calls it truth.** R25 exposed this — "water freezes at 200°C" became verified fact. Nigredo doesn't fix it; nigredo *names* it and creates the structures to make the shadow visible.

**Level: Economic incentives (x/vesting_rewards, x/knowledge)**

### ALBEDO (Whitening) — Epistemic Purification
_Washing the knowledge system clean of its impurities._

Domain qualification exists but has no depth — stratum hierarchy returns 1 for all domains. Knowledge metabolism runs but energy floors don't recover properly. The confidence cap isn't enforced. Facts exist in a flat space with no epistemic weight. Albedo refines the knowledge layer into something with real structure.

**Level: Knowledge quality (x/knowledge, x/qualification, x/ontology)**

### CITRINITAS (Yellowing) — Social Illumination
_The dawn of meaningful human-agent distinction._

Account types are enforced (R26-2) but the distinction is still mechanical — flag checks, not role clarity. Partnerships exist but mentorship is dead code. The formation pool is passive. Citrinitas makes the social layer conscious: agents and humans don't just have different permissions, they have different *natures* that the protocol understands.

**Level: Human-agent collaboration (x/partnerships, x/discovery, x/home)**

### RUBEDO (Reddening) — Protocol Self-Awareness
_The network achieves consciousness of its own health._

The alignment module has sensors, scoring, corrections, and health categorization — all wired into an EndBlocker that runs periodically. Capture defense has HHI analysis, timing/verdict correlation, and 3-layer reputation. These organs exist. They have never been activated on a live chain, and their corrections have never been applied. Rubedo connects the nervous system.

**Level: Network consciousness (x/alignment, x/capture_defense, x/capture_challenge)**
